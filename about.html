About Us

In dhakalgaon people of all castes and religions live together, such as Hindus, Muslims, and people from all castes like SC, ST, OBC and general etc. The majority of the population in this village belongs to the Gurjar community. but apart from this, people of all other castes also live together harmoniously. In social gatherings, people from all castes and religions participate and work together equally. The atmosphere in the village is stable and peaceful.

Gram Panchayat Dhakalgaon

Gram panchayat

Dhakalgaon

Dhakalgao Gram Panchayat is another local body under the Barwah Panchayat Samiti, which is a part of the Khargone Zila Parishad. Dhakalgao Gram Panchayat includes three villages: Nalwa, Birali, and Dhakalgaon. at present, the Sarpanch of the village is Arti Rameshchand, Sachiv of the village is Pannalal Gaindhar and the Rojgar Rahayak is Yashwant Sejgaya.

  • Sarpanch Name : Arti Rameshchand
  • Sachiv : Pannalal Gaindhar
  • Rojgar Sahayak : Yashwant Sejgaya

Major facilitates

Hospital

There is one government hospital in dhakalgaon which provides all kind of services And approx 5-6 private clinics are also available.

School

1 government school and 4 private schools are available in dhakalgaon which provides better education to children.

Water Availability

There is plenty of water available for drinking and irrigation. sources of water like wells, canal, river and tubewells are available.

Transport

Public tansportaion facilities like public buses, auto ricshow and e-ricshow are available to the nearest town sanawad.
